VillageConnect

VillageConnect is a community engagement platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens and local authorities. The platform enables villagers to report local issues, share community updates, access important information, and participate in the development of their village through a simple digital interface.

Features
Report and track local issues
Community announcements and updates
Citizen participation and feedback
Easy-to-use dashboard
Mobile-friendly design
Tech Stack
Base44
HTML, CSS, JavaScript
Responsive UI Design
Goal

To empower rural communities by providing a centralized digital platform for communication, transparency, and collaborative village development.
